What we offer

We bring the best and most affordable accomodation experience to our tenants by offering the peace of mind hustle-free environment needed not only for having a quick and solid start but increase the safety or educational success and your career at large

Kitchen

Our kitchen is spacious and tidy, perfect for preparing your own meals. It has modern stoves, clean fridges, secure food lockers, cooking utensils and storage cupboards. Whether you're an experienced cook or just getting started, our kitchen is set up to make your cooking easy and enjoyable, so you can create delicious meals with ease..

Utilities

Our rental includes essential utilities to make your stay comfortable. You'll have access to Wi-Fi for staying connected, solar power for eco-friendly energy, borehole water for reliable supply, and solar geysers for hot water. Your security is a priority with us, and we provide wardrobes and comfortable beds to make you feel at home during your stay.

Study Room

Our study room is designed to offer a clean and roomy space for your academic needs. It comes equipped with a reading desk and chairs to provide a comfortable study environment. Additionally, fitted cupboards are available to help you organize your study materials efficiently.

About Us

Accommodation Rules

Our accommodation has a set of rules in place to ensure a peaceful and comfortable environment. Smoking is not allowed, and we kindly request that no pets be brought along. Additionally, to maintain a serene atmosphere, we do not permit guests or parties. Our accommodation is exclusively for females, and a one-month deposit is required upon booking.

Common Areas

Regarding our common areas, the owner does not reside on the premises, and there are no pets in the accommodation. We provide a well-equipped kitchen, a cozy living room, and a welcoming dining room for your convenience. You can also enjoy our outdoor area for relaxation. We have three shared bathrooms and four shared toilets to accommodate our residents comfortably.
